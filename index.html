Hi! This is Yibo, currently working toward my Ph.D. degree in Statistics at ZMIC Lab in the School of Data Science, Fudan University, advised by Prof. Xiahai Zhuang. I received a Bachelor of Engineering (B.Eng) degree in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China in the year 2022.

My research mainly focuses on medical image analysis, particularly in interpretable methods for medical image computing and extending them for practical clinical applications.
